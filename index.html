AI Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to systems that perform tasks requiring human-like intelligence—learning, reasoning, decision-making, and language understanding. As AI becomes more integrated into daily life, it’s essential to consider not only what AI can do but the ethical frameworks guiding its use.

Core principles include fairness, transparency, accountability, privacy, and safety. These principles help ensure AI technologies are beneficial, trustworthy, and aligned with human values.
